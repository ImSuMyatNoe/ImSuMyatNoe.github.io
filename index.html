I am Su Myat Noe, a passionate and multidisciplinary researcher in Computer Science, with a specialization in Computer Vision based on AI, Machine Learning and Deep Learning. Currently, I am immersed in pursuing my Third Year Ph.D. at the University of Miyazaki, Japan. My focus lies in developing innovative methods for detecting estrus in cattle using deep learning techniques and machine learning algorithms. Through my publications in renowned conferences and journals, I strive to showcase my expertise and contribute significantly to the field.

I specialize in the application of machine learning and deep learning techniques to address real-world challenges, encompassing the entire spectrum from data collection to model development and evaluation. My research interests primarily revolve around computer vision and data science, with a focus on areas such as object segmentation, multi-camera multi-object tracking, dataset construction, and automatic labeling. Through my expertise, I aim to develop innovative solutions that contribute to advancements in these fields and enable practical applications in various domains.

Beyond academia, I actively engage in leadership and advocacy roles within the AI community. As the Ambassador of Women in AI Myanmar, I work tirelessly to promote gender inclusivity and diversity in the AI domain. Additionally, serving as the Google Developer Student Club Lead of Japan 2022-2023 allows me to connect with fellow AI enthusiasts, providing them with valuable learning opportunities and resources. I find great joy in organizing and speaking at tech events and conferences worldwide, where I share my knowledge and experiences, inspiring others to pursue their aspirations in AI.

I am always keeping a lookout for new gigs. Do you have a perfect gig for me? I am only an email away.
